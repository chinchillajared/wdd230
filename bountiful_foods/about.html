About us

Our History:

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1990 by John Smith, a passionate organic farmer in Carlsbad, California. From a small farm, we grew into a hub for organic and locally sourced fruits and vegetables.

1995: Opened our first storefront in downtown Carlsbad, gaining popularity for quality produce and a genuine connection with customers.

2005: Introduced food trucks, reaching a broader audience along the Southern California coast, from Los Angeles to San Diego.

Today, we remain dedicated to our roots, nourishing the community with fresh, healthy produce and delightful fruit drinks.
